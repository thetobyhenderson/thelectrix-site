Who We Are

Built by Athletes.
Driven by Engineers.

THElectrix is an electronics engineering firm born from the action sports world. Our founding team brings decades of competitive racing, product development, and deep technical expertise — and we build for the athletes and industries we know firsthand.

We operate at the convergence of embedded systems, advanced materials, and real-world performance demands. Every project starts with the same question: what would the athlete actually use? That filter drives everything from circuit design to final integration.

Headquartered in the U.S. with a global development network, we move fast, build smart, and ship technology that performs when it matters most.
